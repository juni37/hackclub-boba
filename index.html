Types of Shellfish

Shellfish can be divided into two categories: crustaceans and mollusks. Common types of shellfish include:

  • Shrimp
  • Crab
  • Lobster
  • Oysters
  • Mussels
  • Clams
  • Scallops

Nutritional Benefits

Shellfish are an excellent source of lean protein, vitamins, and minerals. Some benefits include:

  • High in Omega-3 fatty acids
  • Rich in Vitamin B12
  • Low in calories
  • Good source of iron, zinc, and magnesium

Cooking Tips

Here are some tips to help you cook shellfish perfectly:

  • Always buy fresh shellfish from a reputable source.
  • Keep shellfish cool and cook it as soon as possible after purchase.
  • Avoid overcooking shellfish to prevent it from becoming tough.
  • Use simple seasonings to enhance the natural flavors.
